SPECTRUM WARSAn Iris Demonstration

EPISODE IV

A hands-on introduction to wireless synchronization and dynamic spectrum access.

Spectrum Wars is an interactive game for 4 players using Iris.

Two teams (Red and Blue) compete to establish and maintain a wireless communications link between a transmitter and receiver using the same spectrum band.

The first team to transfer 50000 frames successfully wins!

Each team consists of a transmitter and a receiver. Both transmitter and receiver control their radio by choosing the centre frequency, bandwidth and gain.

Choose to compete by aggressively interfering with the signal of the opposing team or choose to cooperate by avoiding interference and using empty spectrum.

Avoid the primary user signal indicated by the black vertical line on the display. Control your radios using the mouse, arrow keys or hotkeys.

Hotkeys:

Frequency up = Q
Frequency down = A
Bandwidth up = W
Bandwidth down = S
Gain up = E
Gain down = D

Use the display to coordinate with your teammate and navigate the spectrum wars!
